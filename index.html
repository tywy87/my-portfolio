About

Hi! I am Tyler Wyles, a recent graduate of the Juno College of Technology. During my time there, I learned how to build accessible websites using HTML5, CSS3 - SASS, JavaScript, ReactJS, RESTApis and Firebase. I also gained experience with paired programming, Git and Git collaboration, which has helped me become an effective team player.

Before my career in web development, I worked as a real estate coach, teaching new hires the importance of referral relationship building techniques to grow their business. This experience has taught me valuable communication and leadership skills that I now bring to my work as a web developer.

When not coding, I enjoy restoring vintage motorcycles and spending time in nature through hiking and camping. These activities have taught me the importance of patience, attention to detail, and problem-solving, all of which are essential skills in web development.
